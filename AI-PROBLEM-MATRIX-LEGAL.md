# BGS AI Legal and Privacy Matrix

Boundary Governance Suite

Interpretation:
- `Primary BGS members` = member frameworks that directly address the problem
- `Recommended BGS slice` = claimable suite slice when a formal BGS claim is needed

| Problem class | BGS coverage | Already available in a framework? | Primary BGS members | External controls still required | Recommended BGS slice | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Privacy violation | HYBRID | Partially: `BISS`, `UIC`, `UCC` | BISS, UIC, UCC | DLP, privacy gateways, least privilege, review | BGS-Governed | BGS can declare the boundary, not enforce the whole privacy stack. |
